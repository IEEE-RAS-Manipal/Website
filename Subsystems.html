Subsystems of 
IEEE RAS Manipal

Computer Science

The CS subsystem is responsible for the algorithmic aspects of the mechatronic systems pertaining to the sense-think-act paradigm. It will further be bifurcated into perception and planning to work in tandem with the controls vertical. Members will use cutting-edge techniques like AI, computer vision, statistical and probabilistic planning and frameworks like ROS to ease the former.

IT & Development

The IT & Development subsystem supervises and maintains all technological integrations utilised by our organisation, like Linux, GitHub, Google Workspace, Office 365, etc. They also support the other subsystems with the technological know-how require to work with these software. Members will learn Linux, Bash Scripting, Open-Source Software, and Web Development among other essential skills to become an effective SysAdmin

LEARN MORE

Electronics & Control

The electronics subsystem is responsible for the design, creation and management of the systems that integrate with the other subsystems and create an idea of sustainable circuitry to manage the process of automation. Members will use software like LTSpice, Proteus, and Vivado to design circuitry in tandem with coding microcontrollers like the Arduino and Raspberry Pi

LEARN MORE

Mechanical

The Mechanical subsystem is responsible for mechanical design and mathematical modelling & simulation, with their main aim being bringing our endeavours from the drawing board into reality. Members will utilise MATLAB to aid with modelling designs made on Autodesk Fusion 360, among other software like ANSYS, Solidworks etc.

LEARN MORE

Human Resources

Human Resources subsytem is responsible for the entire life cycle of members, which includes recruiting dedicated members, and training them to get the best out of each and every one. The HR is also responsible for maintaining records of all the members at all times. Members will learn how to effectively manage personnel and logistics, create project and technical documentation for our activities, and assist with tracking the finances of the organisation.
