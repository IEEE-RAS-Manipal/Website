About Media & Management Sub-System

Human Resources part of the M&M subsytem is responsible for the entire life cycle of members, which includes recruiting dedicated members, and training them to get the best out of each and every one. The HR is also responsible for maintaining records of all the members at all times. Members will learn how to effectively manage personnel and logistics, create project and technical documentation for our activities, and assist with tracking the finances of the organisation.

The Media and Marketing part of the M&M subsystem is responsible for copywriting, creating content, posts and videos on social media. Promoting the organisation via various social media strategies, to have the maximum reach possible is another major aspect of this subsystem. Members will utilise the Adobe ecosystem (Illustrator, Photoshop, Premier Pro etc.) to create eye-catching media, and create targeted content to publicise on our various social media handles
