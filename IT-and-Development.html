IT AND DEVELOPMENT

The IT & Development subsystem supervises and maintains all technological integrations utilised by our organisation, like Linux, GitHub, Google Workspace, Office 365, etc. They also support the other subsystems with the technological know-how require to work with these software. 

Members will learn Linux, Bash Scripting, Open-Source Software, and Web Development among other essential skills to become an effective SysAdmin
