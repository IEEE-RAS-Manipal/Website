Welcome to IEEE Robotics & Automation Society, Student Chapter Manipal! As a student chapter of one of the largest societies of the Robotics domain, we strive to achieve to advance innovation and applied research in the field of Robotics and Automation.

Each of our core subsystems: Computer Science, Electronics, and Mechanical work independently to advance their respective avenues while working together with each other to collectively further the domain of Robotics and Automation.

We deal with systems incorporating various sensors and actuators that can operate autonomously or semi-autonomously, the intelligence and adaptability to cope with unstructured environments, lastly the efficiency, productivity, and quality, focusing on systems that operate autonomously.

Supporting subsystems assist the core subsystems in achieving their goals. The subsystem of IT & Development upholds the infrastructure of the society, while also maintaining the website, blog, and repositories. Likewise, the subsystem of Human Resources ensures the smooth functioning of the society and its constituent members. Lastly, the subsystem of Media & Marketing is responsible for the content put out by the society for the general public and the industry.
